DiscoJuice end of life

DiscoJuice is no longer maintained, and will be turned entirely off December 31th 2019. The DiscoJuice source code is available on github for those interested, but we would reccommend to migrate to an alternative discovery service.

We appolize for the instability of DiscoJuice the last weeks/months, and the problems that may have caused for your service
